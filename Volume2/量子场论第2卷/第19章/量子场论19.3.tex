},t_{\beta }$等, 与独立的破缺对称性生成元不同, 对于这些生成元, 下标$\alpha $%
取值$a,b$等.}%
\begin{equation}
\sum_{n,m}\frac{\partial V_{0}(\phi )}{\partial \phi _{n}}(t_{\alpha
})_{nm}\phi _{m}=0  \label{19.3.2}
\end{equation}%
\begin{equation}
\left. \frac{\partial V(\phi )}{\partial \phi _{n}}\right\vert _{\phi =\phi
_{0}+\phi _{1}}=0\text{ .}  \label{19.3.3}
\end{equation}%
\begin{equation}
\sum_{m}\left. \frac{\partial ^{2}V_{0}(\phi )}{\partial \phi _{n}\partial
\phi _{m}}\right\vert _{\phi =\phi _{0}}\phi _{1m}+\left. \frac{\partial
V_{1}(\phi )}{\partial \phi _{n}}\right\vert _{\phi =\phi _{0}}=0\text{ .}
\label{19.3.4}
\end{equation}%
\begin{equation}
\sum_{nl}\left. \frac{\partial ^{2}V_{0}(\phi )}{\partial \phi _{n}\partial

.}  \label{19.3.5}
\end{equation}%

\sum_{n}(t_{\alpha }\phi _{0})_{n}\left. \frac{\partial V_{1}(\phi )}{%

\frac{\partial L(\theta )}{\partial \theta ^{\alpha }}L^{-1}(\theta )=%

\end{equation}%
\begin{equation}
\frac{\partial V_{1}(L(\theta )\phi _{\ast })}{\partial \theta _{\alpha }}%

\end{equation}

根据16.1节的一般结果, 
赝Goldstone玻色子直到一阶的质量矩阵$M_{ab}$给定为\begin{equation}
M_{ab}^{2}=\sum_{mn}Z_{an}Z_{bm}\left. \frac{\partial ^{2}V(\phi )}{\partial
\phi _{m}\partial \phi _{n}}\right\vert _{\phi =\phi _{0}+\phi _{1}}\text{ ,}
\label{19.3.10}
\end{equation}%
其中$Z_{an}$是方程(\ref{19.2.39})所定义的场场重整化常数. 既然质量矩阵(\ref{19.3.10})%
在零阶为零, 一阶项给出\begin{equation}
M_{ab}^{2}=\sum_{mn}Z_{an}Z_{bm}\left[ \sum_{\ell }\left. \frac{\partial
^{3}V_{0}(\phi )}{\partial \phi _{\ell }\partial \phi _{m}\partial \phi _{n}}%
\right\vert _{\phi =\phi _{0}}\phi _{1\ell }+\left. \frac{\partial
^{2}V_{1}(\phi )}{\partial \phi _{m}\partial \phi _{n}}\right\vert _{\phi
=\phi _{0}}\right] \text{ ,}  \label{19.3.11}
\end{equation}%
其中$Z_{an}$在这里由(\ref{19.2.40})%
的零阶近似给出:%
\begin{equation}
Z_{an}=\sum_{b}F_{ab}^{-1}(\mathrm{i}t_{b}\phi _{0})_{n}\text{ .}
\label{19.3.12}
\end{equation}%
为了计算质量矩阵(\ref%
{19.3.11}), 我们取方程(\ref{19.2.55})中的$t$为破缺对称性生成元$t_{a}$中的一个, 令$\phi =\phi _{0}$, 
并与$(t_{b}\phi _{0})_{m}\phi _{1\ell }$:%
\begin{eqnarray*}
0 &=&\sum_{nm\ell }\left. \frac{\partial ^{3}V_{0}(\phi )}{\partial \phi
_{\ell }\partial \phi _{m}\partial \phi _{n}}\right\vert _{\phi =\phi
_{0}}\phi _{1\ell }(t_{a}\phi _{0})_{n}(t_{b}\phi _{0})_{m} \\
&&+\sum_{nm}\left. \frac{\partial ^{2}V_{0}(\phi )}{\partial \phi
_{n}\partial \phi _{m}}\right\vert _{\phi =\phi _{0}}(t_{a}\phi
_{1})_{n}(t_{b}\phi _{0})_{m}+\sum_{n\ell }\left. \frac{\partial
^{2}V_{0}(\phi )}{\partial \phi _{n}\partial \phi _{\ell }}\right\vert
_{\phi =\phi _{0}}(t_{a}t_{b}\phi _{0})_{n}\phi _{1\ell }\text{ .}
\end{eqnarray*}%
根据方程(\ref{19.2.6}), 右边的第二项为零, 而第三项可以利用方程(\ref{19.3.4})进行重写, 这给了我们\begin{equation}
\sum_{nm\ell }\left. \frac{\partial ^{3}V_{0}(\phi )}{\partial \phi _{\ell
}\partial \phi _{m}\partial \phi _{n}}\right\vert _{\phi =\phi _{0}}\phi
_{1\ell }(t_{a}\phi _{0})_{n}(t_{b}\phi _{0})_{m}=\left. \frac{\partial
V_{1}(\phi )}{\partial \phi _{n}}\right\vert _{\phi =\phi
_{0}}(t_{a}t_{b}\phi _{0})_{n}\text{ .}  \label{19.3.13}
\end{equation}%
这样, 在方程(\ref{19.3.11})使用上式就给出了赝Goldstone玻色子的质量矩阵公式, 
以$V_{1}$的形式, 这个公式是\begin{eqnarray}
M_{cd}^{2} &=&-\sum_{ab}F_{ca}^{-1}F_{db}^{-1}[(t_{a}\phi
_{0})_{n}(t_{b}\phi _{0})_{m}\left. \frac{\partial ^{2}V_{1}(\phi )}{%
\partial \phi _{m}\partial \phi _{n}}\right\vert _{\phi =\phi _{0}} 
\nonumber \\
&&+(t_{a}t_{b}\phi _{0})_{n}\left. \frac{\partial V_{1}(\phi )}{\partial
\phi _{n}}\right\vert _{\phi =\phi _{0}}]\text{ .}  \label{19.3.14}
\end{eqnarray}

为了使它是一个合理的质量矩阵, 它最好是正定的. 为了看到这一点, 将这一结果重写成对群参量$\theta _{\alpha }$的导数形式将是方便的. 对方%
程(\ref{19.3.8})做相对$\theta _{\beta }$的微分, 令$\theta =\theta _{\ast }$, 并使用%
方程(\ref{19.3.9})和$\phi _{0}=L(\theta _{\ast })\phi
_{\ast }$, 这给出\begin{equation}
M_{ab}^{2}=\sum_{cd\alpha \beta }N_{a\alpha }^{-1}(\theta _{\ast })N_{b\beta
}^{-1}(\theta _{\ast })F_{ac}^{-1}F_{bd}^{-1}\left. \frac{\partial
^{2}V_{1}(L(\theta )\phi _{\ast })}{\partial \theta _{\alpha }\partial
\theta _{\beta }}\right\vert _{\theta =\theta _{\ast }}\text{ .}
\label{19.3.15}
\end{equation}%
由于$\theta _{\ast }$是函数$V_{1}(L(\theta
)\phi _{\ast })$的{\kai{最小值点}}, 右边的矩阵是正定的.

(算符$\Phi _{n}$不必须是基本标量场), 这构成了生成元为$t_{\alpha }$的对称群的一个表示, 也就是说\begin{equation}

\end{equation}%
